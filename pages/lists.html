weekday+lists. To keep your calendar clean, you can toggle each weekday on and off depending on what is important to you. Simple use the W button in the app toggle off irrelevant weekdays.
In addition to the calendar, you also have access to a variety of lists to keep track of tasks and notes. The available lists are someday, project, personal and work.
You can switch between the lists using the L button in the app or by using the hotkeys.
